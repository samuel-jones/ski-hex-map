Methods

Based on data from OpenStreetMap © contributors and downloaded from www.opensnowmap.org. Additional queries based around keywords ('piste' and 'route=>ski') were then applied. The data has been aggregated to a celluar grid with each hexagon cell having an area of around 2, 500km². Aggregation is based on the sum of lengths for each polyline within a hexagon cell and was calculated within QGIS. This has been classified based on a rounded natural Jenks classification using seven different colours. The height of each hexagon is based on the caluclated total length in metres.
